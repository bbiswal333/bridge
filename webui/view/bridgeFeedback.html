We love your feedback!


Please let us know what can be improved and what you like about Bridge. Therefore, please provide us detailed information, e.g. include screenshots whenever possible.

In case you want to report a bug we'd ask you to refresh your browser first (Win: F5, Mac: CMD + R) and try to reproduce it. We are shipping new versions of Bridge frequently and have experienced browser-caching issues especially with IE and Firefox.

We have 2 feedback channels:

1) If you are already familiar with GitHub, please do not hesitate to create an issue in our GitHub Project. We also look forward to any pull request from you in order to extend & enhance this application.

2) Alternatively, you can mail us ;-).
